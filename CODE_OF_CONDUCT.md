# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Showing empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing for mistakes

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment or intimidation of any kind
- Discrimination, derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without permission
- Sustained disruption of discussions

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces and public communications related to the project.

## Enforcement
Report issues by opening a private security advisory or contacting the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

## Enforcement Guidelines
- **Correction**: A private, written warning with guidance.
- **Warning**: A formal warning with consequences for continued behavior.
- **Temporary Ban**: A temporary ban from project spaces.
- **Permanent Ban**: A permanent ban for sustained or severe violations.
